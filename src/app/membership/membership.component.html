Fees

The fee for each Mikvah visit is $26.00. Appointments made online pay an additional $2 fee.

Payments made at the time of your visit can be made by cash or check. When making an appointment online payments may be made via credit or debit card.

Annual membership payments can be made online via credit or debit card. See below for information on joining.

Membership

Membership is an important way to help support the mikvah.

There is no individual use fee for Mikvah members.

There are four different levels of membership available to show your support:

  • $360 Standard Membership

  • $500 Silver Membership

  • $1000 Gold Membership

  • $1800 Platinum Membership
